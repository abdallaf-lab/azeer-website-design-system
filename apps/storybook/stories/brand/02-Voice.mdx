# Voice & Tone — صوت البراند ونبرة الصوت

> صوت البراند هو شخصيته ظاهرةٌ في كلامه.

Azeer's voice is **Rooted (راسخ)**, a **Supportive ally (موآزر)**, and **Precise
(دقيق)**. Each attribute scales across three volume levels — Quiet (خافت),
Medium (متوسط), Loud (عالي) — so the same personality fits a tooltip, an email,
or a campaign headline.

<VoiceAttributes />

<Callout intent="info" title="Language by surface">
Use **Modern Standard Arabic (الفصحى)** in documents, email, and the website.
Use the **neutral Saudi 'white' dialect** on social media and ad campaigns. Other
Arabic dialects are allowed only in specific contexts — consult the brand owner
first.
</Callout>
