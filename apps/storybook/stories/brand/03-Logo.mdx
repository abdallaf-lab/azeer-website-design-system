# Logo — الشعار

The Azeer mark fuses a **Reuleaux triangle** (constant-width geometry →
stability and reliability) with **sand waves** (flow and adaptability across
channels). Rendered live below from the `<AzeerLogo>` component.

<LogoLockups />

<Callout intent="info" title="Sizing">
The lockup scales with `font-size`: the mark and wordmark use `em` units, so
`<AzeerLogo className="text-3xl" />` resizes the whole lockup proportionally.
Always preserve aspect ratio — hold **Shift** when resizing in design tools.
Clear space: keep at least **2x** around the logo (x = the construction-grid unit).
</Callout>

## Variants — أشكال الشعار

`<AzeerLogo tone="…">` covers the approved color treatments:

- `color` — indigo mark + navy wordmark (light surfaces)
- `inverse` — all white (dark surfaces / `bg-brand-secondary`)
- `mono` — single `currentColor` (caller sets it)
- `black` — near-black neutral

`variant` controls the lockup: `horizontal` (default), `stacked`, or `mark`
(symbol only). `wordmark` picks `both` (default), `ar`, or `en`.

## What not to do

<LogoRulesLists />

<Callout intent="danger" title="Protect the mark">
Don't recolor, restyle, skew, add elements, build sub-logos, drop shadows, or
reproduce the logo at low quality. Keep maximum contrast with the background and
never place the color logo on a dark color or a busy image.
</Callout>
