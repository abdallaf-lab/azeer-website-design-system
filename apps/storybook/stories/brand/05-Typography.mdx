# Typography — الخطوط

The brand display typeface is **Dialogue ME** — a dual-script (Arabic + Latin)
family that reads as modern and renewed while keeping authenticity, and speaks
to an Arab audience with a local personality.

<BrandTypeSpecimen />

<Callout intent="warning" title="Implementation note">
**Dialogue ME is not bundled in this repo.** The specimen above renders in the
product fonts (Inter / IBM Plex Sans Arabic). For production marketing surfaces,
self-host the licensed Dialogue ME and map it onto `--font-sans` / `--font-arabic`
on the marketing site. Until then, the product fonts are the implemented fallback.
</Callout>

<Callout intent="info" title="Weights">
Dialogue ME ships seven weights: Extralight, Light, Regular, Medium, Demibold,
Bold, Extrabold. Note the product UI (Foundation → Typography) deliberately
restricts itself to three weights (400 / 500 / 600) for app density — the wider
brand range is for marketing/editorial use.
</Callout>

In code: `import { BRAND_TYPOGRAPHY } from "@azeer/tokens"` exposes the family,
scripts, tagline, and weight list.
