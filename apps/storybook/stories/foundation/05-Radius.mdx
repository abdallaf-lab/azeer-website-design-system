# Radius

**Six values, locked.** `md` (6px) is the system default. **12px is the
ceiling** — no 16, 20, 24. Modal corner = card corner = 12px. Hierarchy comes
from elevation, not from radius.

<RadiusScale />

<Callout intent="danger" title="Forbidden">
- `rounded-[Npx]` arbitrary values
- `rounded-2xl` (24px) or larger — capped at 12px
- 4 / 10 / 14 / 16 / 20 / 24 px custom radii
- Tailwind default `rounded-sm` (which is 2px in Tailwind, but our `rounded-sm` is **4px**)
</Callout>

## Component → radius mapping

| Surface | Radius | Why |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Buttons, inputs, list items | `rounded-md` (6px) | System default |
| Chips, badge dots, checkboxes | `rounded-sm` (4px) | Tiny surfaces — small radius for crispness |
| Dropdowns, popovers, tooltips, inner cards | `rounded-lg` (8px) | Floating surfaces — slightly softer |
| Cards, modals, sheets, shell panels | `rounded-xl` (12px) | Ceiling — large surfaces, calm curvature |
| Avatars (people), switches, pills, status dots | `rounded-full` | Circular by intent |
| Avatars (organizations) | `rounded-lg` (8px) | Distinguishes "company" from "person" at a glance |
