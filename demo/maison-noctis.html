— II —

A method of three patiences.

We do not work to a brief. Each composition begins on a Tuesday in autumn, with a single material laid on the bench, and ends — sometimes years later — on the morning it ceases to surprise its maker.

i. Materia

Raw, never reconstituted.

Absolutes, resins, and tinctures sourced directly from growers — Bulgaria, Calabria, Mysore, Madagascar. No analogues, no captives, no shortcuts dressed in Latin.

ii. Maceration

Six months in glass.

Every batch matures in amber glass, untouched, for at least a hundred and eighty days. We wait until the formula stops asking questions of itself.

iii. Modesty

No campaign, no influencer.

Discovery is by letter, by friend, or by happenstance in our atelier on Rue de Sévigné. The work is the advertisement — there is no other.
